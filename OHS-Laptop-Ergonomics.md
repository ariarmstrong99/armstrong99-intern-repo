What are the risks of using a laptop without an external monitor and keyboard?

 On a long term basis, using a laptop without an external monitor and without keyboard can cause issues with posture and lead to issues with back pain. This is because you are forced to look downward and the keyboard that is attached is not possible to adjust into an ergonomic position. This can cause issues with neck pain, back pain, lead to a more rounded posture, cause issues with eye strain and even lead to carpal tunnel type symptoms. Using an external keyboard, a laptop stand and an external mouse allows the screen to be properly adjusted according to one’s own needs and raise to eye level. The arms can stay in a more natural and relaxed position as well.
