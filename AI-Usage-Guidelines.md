What are the benefits and risks of using AI in a professional setting? 

The main benefits of using AI in a professional setting are that it has the power to reduce the time taken to finish a given work task. 
This is only applicable for tasks that are quite tedious and relative in nature. 
On the other hand, AI can allow designers to work more efficiently by allowing them to visualise fairly quickly multiple iterations of the digital product that they are currently designing. 
In this manner, designers can come up with a product that has an exemplary user experience whilst also ensuring it provides significant business value. 
Ai can be used by designers to give them more direction in the brainstorming process when it is not possible to hold a design workshop. 
The fundamental issue with ai is that it cannot produce original content. It is also producing outputs that are very generic in nature and has the tendency to “hallucinate” by providing information that is entirely inaccurate. 
Ai is not sentient and does not have an imagination. This means that there are issues with privacy and confidentiality if any user information is provided to an ai tool such as ChatGPT. 
Relying extensively on ai can reduce the ability to think critically and also lower the standard of work outputs. 
Ai is only useful in a professional context to augment current capabilities in order to speed up the work process to help designers be more efficient with their work. 


What types of information should never be entered into AI tools? 

Any type of confidential information should not be entered into an ai tool. For example:

Personal information
User imformation and any internal information about the organisation that is confidential and private
Any internal company documentation or data
Any information about the product, feature or design that is not public information
Source code that is proprietary in nature
Any company documents and financial information 



How can you fact-check and validate AI-generated content to ensure accuracy? 

Ai generated content has a tendency to be inaccurate,and  generic in nature. The term “ai slop” specifically refers to this phenomenon of ai tools churning out content that is very one dimensional in nature, with accuracy often being highly inconsistent. 
It is always important to review ai generated outputs when using an ai tool. It is Important to verify the information by doing independent research and checking the original sources that are cited by ai. 
If ai provides factual imformation, I would ask the tool for official sources and verify this information by going through the original sources.

How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it? 

Ai tools cannot be relied upon. They are significantly prone to making mistakes and providing false information. 
In addition, they are tools that augment existing capabilities that a person already possesses. 
An ai tool can help a person enhance their learning process, but it cannot make them learn. 
Similarly an ai tool cannot do a persons work or help them outsource their thinking process. 
Ai tools are best used for select tasks that are repetitive in natrue. 
Such as during the process of producing multiple iterations at a very baseline level for a product or app I am already working on and have already created designs for. 
I can ask the ai tool to generate various interactions without feeding my designs and it will produce iterations that help me visualise things better. 
But it does not mean those iterations are “final”. Those iterations must be improved on significantly, but it helps speed up my process massively.
