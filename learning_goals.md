What do you hope to learn from this internship?

I hope to gain a deeper understanding of how I can execute and think like a strong product designer. I have always been interested on solving a real world problem in the area of mental health or healthtech. 
It would be very beneficial for my growth as a product designer to learn more about designing for groups that have differential needs, such  as individuals that are neurodivergent and ones with accessibility requirements. 
There are a lot of assumptions in product design and UX design that simply do not apply to people with adhd. I would like to be in a position to challenge those assumptions so that I can create a product in the future that does not alienate this demographic. 
Being able to design successfully for neurodivergent populations and others with accessibility needs is a unique skills set that will invariably separate me from other designers.
Ultimately, I would like to cultivate the skills, get exposure to digital products that solve similar problems in the area of mental health, wellness and healthtech. My current internship with focus bear will help me observe exactly how real decisions are made. 
It will also allow me to gain more insights into the exact reasons behind why particular decisions are made in a lean startup environment such  as focus bear.
Being able to observe how the team iterates, validates assumptions, formulates hypothesis and then executes under high pressure to not only attract more users, but scale the business will allow me to become a better product designer that is more equipped at launching my own startup to solve a real world complex problem.
